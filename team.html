Why are we doing this

The area I want to explore with this project is drug abuse and overdoses. The issues surrounding drug abuse have become mainstream with the opioid epidemic, and the realization that rather than this being a moral issue it's a social issue.

This shift in mindset is fascinating and I think it'd be interesting to do an independent survey of the field of drug use, abuse, and overdosage in the midst of a changing regulation landscape. The current information in the field is still fresh, and while opioids dominate headlines, there are many drugs that have been overlooked. In addition the legalization of marijuana has become common across North America.

The importance of the data being fresh is two fold, one there isn't a lot of reliable studies of the data, and second the studies and visualizations are in no way guaranteed to be presenting fully valid findings. This data with new regulations is far too new to see broad impacts, however setting up the infrastructure to track and see the changes would be interesting if possible.
